My Personality

Hello everyone! I am Terry, and I am currently a full-time diploma student studying Applied AI and Analytics at Singapore Polytechnic. My personality has been changing a lot throughout the different phases of my life. In my primary school life, I was extremely playful and mischievous, always causing trouble for my teachers. In my secondary school life, I was rather quiet and introverted. I always kept to a small group of friends and avoided the spotlight. And now, in my polytechnic life, I have changed again. I did the MBTI personality test not too long ago, and my result was ENTJ-T, which stands for extraverted, intuitive, thinking, judging and turbulent:

Extraverted
I view myself as a confident and outgoing person. I love taking charge in group situations and always enjoy others' company. I love seeking out social simulations and opportunities to talk to others. My friends often describe me as ‚ÄúFull of energy and positivity‚Äù.
Intuitive
Exploring new ideas and possibilities in every situation is basically a norm for me. I would always think a few steps ahead in the things I'm doing and would always be focused on the bigger picture.
Thinking
Before making decisions, I would first analyze and evaluate the given information. When challenged by others, I would use evidence and objective reasoning based on facts and data to make a rational decision.
Judging
I prefer things to be settled and crossed off my list whenever possible. And how I do so is by having order, planning and organization in my life and work. I prefer to weigh out my options ahead of time so that I can have clarity and closure. Which is why I like to stick with a plan rather than going with the flow.
Turbulent
As the word 'turbulent' suggests, I could be in a state of agitation or disturbed when something doesn't go my way. I tend to be more sensitive to stress and more self-critical than others. It might seem like a bad thing; but I treat this as a blessing in disguise. I would use these feeling and thought to push myself to do better!

My Values

Values are an essential part of who I am and what I stand for. Through my life experiences, relationships and hardships, I came to realise my 4 life values that I live by. Hover over each picture to understand why i chose each picture!

Integrity

Integrity and honesty might mean the same to you, but it is different to me. Someone can have honesty without integrity but he or she cannot have integrity without honesty. I have a strong moral principle based on honesty and always do what is right. I aim to demonstrate my integrity through my behavior and decision-making.

Kindness

An act of simple kindness doesn't cost anything or take much time. This simple act of kindness allows me make someone's day. It makes me happy to see someone being grateful towards me and I believe it has the power to create positive change, improve relationships, and contribute to a more caring and harmonious society.

Commitment

I strongly believe that personal growth and learning are the core of being successful. Personal growth and learning are lifelong endeavors, I will embrace the process, stay committed to my goals and celebrate my progress along the way. This will allow me to be the best version of myself, giving me the necessary confidence.

Courage

Courage is an important value that I live by. Courage can be shown in different ways; Be it owning up to mistakes that I have done or confessing to someone I like. Courage is important to me because, in times of hardships, we need the courage to step forward, persevere and have the mental fortitude to uphold these values.
