Singapore Polytechnic

"By unifying our practices, curricula, partnerships and people, we safeguard the health and well-being of our students, staff and neighbours‚Äîto build a more sustainable future for everyone." ~SP

Singapore Polytechnic has created an Environmental Policy that supports environmental sustainability. They are committed to caring for the environment by going green and fostering environmental sustainability. They are doing actions such as controlling pollution via the 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) and complying with environmental legislation and other requirements. They came up with SP GREEN pledge:


Green our campus,
Reduce, Reuse and Recycle,
Educate ourselves on green issues,
Encourage others to do likewise,
Nurture a love for our environment.

SP Pledges to Progress

4
Green Mark Platinum Buildings

T18A, T18B, T3B and the SP Sports Arena have been certified Green Mark Platinum with on-going efforts to certify all 22 buildings in SP by 2025.

230
Species of Flora

SP's lush campus is home to an incredible biodiversity of plants including our majestic Brown Woolly Fig Heritage Trees found near Food Court 1.

110+
Water Dispensers

We cultivate eco-conscious habits by encouraging students and staff to use water dispensers around campus and have ceased the use of plastic bottled water at our events.

500+
Smart Sensors

Many facilities on campus are monitored with smart sensors to track human traffic and monitor air quality for the health and well-being of students and staff.

4
Smart Offices

Enhancing the work environment for the health and well-being of our staff through air quality and temperature sensors in our offices.

13
Diplomas featuring Common Core Curriculum (CCC)

The CCC prepares our students for the green economy by exposing them to inter-disciplinary learning experiences and global issues, framed by the SG Green Plan and the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals, where they will learn to develop prototype sustainability solutions for real-life issues.

SP Environmental Club

Here at Singapore Polytechnic, the SP Environment Club is a CCA that was created specifically to educate and promote good environmentally-friendly practices through fun activities. The club is committed to promoting environmental awareness and a sustainable lifestyle both on campus and outside. They have organised many green campaigns and projects meant to make a difference in SP, which include roadshows and out-of-school trails!
